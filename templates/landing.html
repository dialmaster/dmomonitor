DMO Monitor Free (Beta)

The always free-to-use DMO Monitor (beta) is now available here to help with your Dynamo monitoring needs. Create an account and use dmo-wrapminer to run your miners to monitor your miners and mining stats from anywhere!

During beta your feedback is greatly appreciated. Mmail me any issues you have, how you would like to use this, or how it could be changed to meet your needs at cdial@dmo-monitor.com

Please note that if you choose to use the beta, you will need to update your dmo-wrapminer when the full management capability is released.

Currently this features support for:
  • Monitoring of your miners and their hashrates for DynMiner v2.06+
  • Notifications if a miner goes offline, via Telegram
  • Display of mining statistics such as coins per day, coins per hour for solo mining
  • Native mining wrapper app for both Windows and Linux
Note that there is an older version of dmo-monitor, which has been deprecated and will no longer be updated or supported, which is run as a local service. You can still get it here. Binaries are available for both Linux and Windows.
